Smartphone Bodycam

This demo streams live video from a phone's camera to this server, which runs real-time object detection (PPE / fire & smoke) and shows the annotated feed to anyone watching.

📱 Broadcast (phone with the camera)

  1. Open the Broadcast link below on the phone that will act as the camera.
  2. Choose a detection model (PPE, Fire/Smoke, or Both).
  3. Tap "Start Broadcast" and allow camera access when prompted.
Go to Broadcast

Only one phone can broadcast at a time.

🖥️ View (anyone watching)

Open the View link below to watch the live annotated feed. No camera or special permissions needed — this works on any device, including a laptop or a second phone.

Go to View

Nothing shows up on View until a broadcast is active.
